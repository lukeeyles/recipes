No Bake Protein Bars

Ingredients

  • 2 cups oats
  • 4 scoops (120 g) protein powder
  • 1/2 cup (130 g) peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tbsp golden syrup

Directions

  1. Combine all ingredients and refrigerate/freeze.
