Chocolate Protein Pudding

1x

Adapted from this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups + 0.5 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder (sifted)
  • 2 scoops (60 g) protein powder (sifted)
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 50 g dark chocolate (optional)

Instructions

  1. Heat the 2 cups milk in a small saucepan.
  2. Sift in the cocoa powder and protein powder, and add the salt.
  3. Cornstarch slurry: Meanwhile, combine the 1/2 cup milk with the 3 tbsp cornstarch and whisk together.
  4. Boil: When the 2 cups milk are hot, add the cornstarch slurry and bring to the boil.
  5. Stir for 2 mins while boiling, then reduce heat and stir for 1 more minute.
  6. Chocolate: Add in the chocolate if using, stir to melt and incorporate.
  7. Refrigerate until cold. Alternatively freeze and stir frequently to make ice cream.
