Chili Con Carne

1x

Based on Downshiftology's recipe

Ingredients

Main ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 300 g beef mince
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 400 g tomato can
  • 400 g black beans can
  • 1 bay leaf

Spice mix

  • 2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 tsp smoky paprika
  • 1 1/2 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp chili

Instructions

  1. Onion: Cook onion in a frying pan until soft.
  2. Garlic: Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
  3. Beef: Add beef and brown.
  4. Spices: Add spices and toast a bit.
  5. Simmer: Add stock, canned tomato, beans, and bay leaf and simmer.
