Apple Crumble

Based on RecipeTinEats apple crumble.

Ingredients

Filling

  • 1 kg granny smith apples
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1/4 cup white or brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice, or water plus 1/2 tsp citric acid
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp nutmeg

Topping

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup loosely packed brown sugar (note: can reduce down to 1/2 cup depending on sweetness preference)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  • 125 g butter, melted
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (both fan and standard).
  2. Apples: Peel apples and slice into small pieces.
  3. Apple filling: Mix apple filling ingredients together and place in a pie dish.
  4. Crumble topping: Mix crumble topping together and spread over the apples.
  5. Bake 40 minutes until golden brown. Rest for 10 minutes before serving.
