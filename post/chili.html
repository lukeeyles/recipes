Chili

Ingredients

Main ingredients

  • 1 onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 300g beef mince
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 400g tomato can
  • 400g black beans can
  • 1 bay leaf

Spice mix

  • 2 tsp sweet paprika
  • 2 tsp smoky paprika
  • 1.5 tsp cumin
  • 0.5 tsp oregano
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.25 tsp chili

Directions

  1. Cook onion
  2. Cook garlic
  3. Cook beef
  4. Add spices and toast a bit
  5. Add stock, canned tomato, tomato paste, beans, and bay leaf
