## No Bake Protein Bars

### Ingredients

* _2_ cups oats
* _4_ scoops (_120_ g) protein powder
* _1/2_ cup (_130_ g) peanut butter
* _1/2_ cup milk
* _2_ tbsp golden syrup

### Directions

1. Combine all ingredients and refrigerate/freeze.
