## Apple Crumble

Based on [RecipeTinEats apple crumble](https://www.recipetineats.com/apple-crumble/).

### Ingredients
#### Filling
* _1_ kg granny smith apples
* _1_ tbsp flour
* _1/4_ cup white or brown sugar
* _2_ tbsp lemon juice, or water plus _1/2_ tsp citric acid
* _1/2_ tsp cinnamon
* _1/4_ tsp nutmeg

#### Topping
* _1_ cup rolled oats
* _1_ cup flour
* _1_ cup loosely packed brown sugar
* _1/2_ tsp baking powder
* _1_ tsp cinnamon
* _1/2_ tsp nutmeg
* _125_ g butter, melted
* Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. **Preheat** oven to 180°C (both fan and standard).
2. **Apples**: Peel apples and slice into small pieces.
3. **Apple filling**: Mix apple filling ingredients together and place in a pie dish. 
4. **Crumble topping**: Mix crumble topping together and spread over the apples.
5. **Bake** 40 minutes until golden brown. Rest for 10 minutes before serving.
