## Chili Con Carne

Based on [Downshiftology's recipe](https://downshiftology.com/recipes/best-chili-recipe/)

### Ingredients

#### Main ingredients

* _1_ onion
* _4_ garlic cloves
* _300_ g beef mince
* _1_ cup chicken stock
* _400_ g tomato can 
* _400_ g black beans can
* _1_ bay leaf

#### Spice mix

* _2_ tsp sweet paprika
* _2_ tsp smoky paprika
* _1 1/2_ tsp cumin
* _1/2_ tsp oregano
* _1/2_ tsp salt
* _1/4_ tsp chili

### Instructions

1. **Onion**: Cook onion in a frying pan until soft.
2. **Garlic**: Add garlic and cook until fragrant.
3. **Beef**: Add beef and brown.
4. **Spices**: Add spices and toast a bit.
5. **Simmer**: Add stock, canned tomato, beans, and bay leaf and simmer.
