## Chocolate Protein Pudding

Adapted from [this recipe](https://chocolatecoveredkatie.com/healthy-chocolate-pudding-recipe/).

### Ingredients

* 2 cups + 1/2 cup milk
* 1/4 cup cocoa powder (sifted)
* 2 scoops (60g) protein powder (sifted)
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 3 tbsp cornstarch
* 1 tsp vanilla extract
* 50g dark chocolate (optional)

### Directions

1. Add the 2 cups milk, cocoa powder, protein powder, and salt into a small pot.
2. Turn on heat and stir to combine.
3. Meanwhile, combine the 1/2 cup milk with the 3 tbsp cornstarch and whisk together. 
4. When the 2 cups milk are hot, add the 1/2 cup milk and cornstarch mixture and bring to the boil.
5. Stir for 2 mins while boiling, then reduce heat and stir for 1 more minute.
6. Add in the chocolate if using, stir to melt and incorporate.
7. Refrigerate until cold. Alternatively freeze and stir frequently to make ice cream.
