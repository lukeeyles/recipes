## Chili

### Ingredients

#### Main ingredients

* _1_ onion
* _4_ garlic cloves
* _300_ g beef mince
* _1_ cup chicken stock
* _1_ tbsp tomato paste
* _400_ g tomato can 
* _400_ g black beans can
* _1_ bay leaf

#### Spice mix

* _2_ tsp sweet paprika
* _2_ tsp smoky paprika
* _1 1/2_ tsp cumin
* _1/2_ tsp oregano
* _1/2_ tsp salt
* _1/4_ tsp chili

### Directions

1. Cook onion
2. Cook garlic
3. Cook beef
4. Add spices and toast a bit
5. Add stock, canned tomato, tomato paste, beans, and bay leaf
